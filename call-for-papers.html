Call for Papers

We invite submissions to the Universality and Dynamics in High-Dimensional Learning and Inference Workshop at ISIT 2026. This workshop aims to bring together researchers from information theory, machine learning, high-dimensional statistics, random matrix theory, and statistical physics to develop a unified view of recent theoretical advances in high-dimensional learning and inference.

For detailed information on submission guidelines, important dates, topics of interest, travel grants, and awards, please refer to the official Call for Papers document below.
